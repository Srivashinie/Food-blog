Seasonal Tastes Review

Rating: 4/5

Cuisine:Indian, Asian, Vegetarian Friendly

Price: Rs.1000 to Rs.1500

Address: 154 Velachery Main Road, Chennai

Seasonal Tastes offers an amazing variety of food! They have live stations with delicious options like sushi, dim sum, gazpacho, noodles, pastas, roesti, and cold stone ice cream. You can also enjoy vibrant salads, tasty cold cuts, a diverse main course buffet with dishes from different cuisines, and a delightful dessert buffet. It's a fantastic and diverse dining experience for everyone.

The Reef Review

Rating: 4.5/5

Cuisine:Indian, Asian, International

Price: Rs.500 to Rs.1500

Address: 280 ECR, Vadanamelli Sheraton Grand Chennai Resort & Spa, Chennai

From the aromatic biryani to the rich tapestry of Chinese delights, from specialties fresh from the sea to the comforting simplicity of dal chawal, it's a culinary adventure all under one roof. Take a moment to explore, indulge your taste buds in a delightful array, and relish the diverse offerings. The food is truly finger-licking good. The ambiance is exquisite, and the service is top-notch. The staff's courtesy and friendliness enhance the overall dining experience, making each bite more enjoyable.

The Flying Elephant Review

Rating: 4/5

Cuisine:Asian, Italian, Indian, International

Price: Rs.2000 to Rs.2500

Address: No 39 Velachery Main Road, Near Raj Bhavan, Chennai

The Flying Elephant is a multifaceted sensory exploration of recipes that go beyond culinary limits. An elevated adventure that pushes geographical and gastronomical boundaries and picks them apart with gusto. At the centre of this experience is a menu that brings together ideas, ingredients and techniques from across the world, leaving you enchanted, transformed and uplifted all at once.It's 'fun dining' designed and delivered by a team of professionals with an eye for detail and a penchant for discovering.

C Salt Review

Rating: 4.5/5

Cuisine:Indian, Seafood, Asian, Healthy

Price: Rs.1500 to Rs.3000

Address: 280, East Coast Rd, Chennai

C Salt has a rustic and pleasant ambience with comfortable seating and captivating music that elevates the overall dining experience. The specially curated ‘sea to plate’ fresh seafood menu reflects the personal notes on what comes out of the coastal belt of Southern India, with a focus on locally sourced ingredients and flavours. This theme flows across the menu
